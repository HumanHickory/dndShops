Academy of Mystic Arts

An organization dedicated to bringing magic users together, and empowering them in ways that are unachievable by ones self. Although all magic users are welcome, certain magic users, such as Paladins and Arcane Trickers may find it challenging to move up through the ranks. However, for those such as wizards, warlocks, druids, sorcerers, and bards, the Academy is a wonderous place to put your current skills to the test, and learn something new in the process.

Knights of the Lost Age

Our guild is currently seeking men and women who enjoy and excel at combat. Combat may include hand-to-hand, weapondry, mounted combat, magical combat and more. We require a strength of at least 12 to join, and recommend members have a constitution of at least 11. Come show us what you've got!

Coalition Dedicated to the Eradication of the Undead

CDEU is a temporary guild that has been set up by the senate of Sonridge to combat the undead problem. All who wish to kill the undead and keep their fellow citizens alive may apply to join.
