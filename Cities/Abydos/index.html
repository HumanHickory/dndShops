History of Abydos

We are the first to call the Red Desert home. Our human ancestors settled here. Even as mere humans, they were extraordinary. They carved our home out of the Storm's Rage Mountains, at the base of the higest peak, into the red rock of our desert home. Our fiendish ancestors climbed their way out of the Abyss, a nearly impossible feat. They found the mountainside settlement and claimed it as their own. Our human ancestors, although impressive by human standards, were forced to submit to the devils, and those who were fortunate enough to survive, found themselves in positions of servitude, often carrying our forefathers unwillingly for their masters. These half-fiends continued the tradition of slavery, often mating with their slaves. The union of these created our kind. The Tieflings.

Many generations passed. Our fiendish ancestors were either vanquished by warriors, killed by their sons, or returned to the Abyss to seek extraordinary power. Our home became free of masters. Slavery will never enter Abydos again to honor our mothers who nurtured us unwillingly. However, to honor our fathers, who gave us power beyond what any human might possess, we continue the tradition of slavery by selling those who have wronged our home to the Spider Queen.
