The City and it's History

At the center of Caster Hills, Arabelle stood as the primary source of magical power in Avyngäard. Wizards, sorcerers, warlocks, and even clerics and druids would flock to our magical city for a chance to learn ancient secrets and learn from the most powerful magic users in the world.

However, times have changed. Due to the magical ban across the continent, Arabelle has agreed to outlaw magic, to continue dipolmatic relations with our sister cities. Since Sir Ramsley Narrowbush and his ancestors have come to rule over Arabelle, we have seen a dramatic decrease in all things magic in Arabelle, including the sales of magic promoting items. These are the golden years of Arabelle.

At the center of Caster Hills, Arabelle continues to be the primary source of magical power in Avyngäard, and provides a haven to all magical users who wish to use practice their craft without the legal implications they may face in other parts of the world.

All magic users, enthusiasts, and friends are welcome in Arabelle. Congratulations on finding us and we hope you enjoy your stay!
