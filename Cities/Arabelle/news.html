Weekly News

Sonridge Tremors

Severe earthquakes have been radiating from Sonridge. Officials report that they have ceased mining and the Tremors are still occuring. Experts from the Church of the Herald suspect that magic users in Undrek'Thoz, the Drow City, may be behind it, but leadership in Sonridge say this is unlikely, because the tremors are also affecting their city.

It seems these earthquakes are being formed well below the city, deep in the Underdark. No one has dared venture so deep, but if the miners of Sonridge do not discover the source of these tremors soon, it could be the end of live as we know it on the eastern side of the continent.

Church of the Herald Recruiting Now!

The Church of the Herald, gaining unexpected popularity throughout Avyngäard, has decided to increase its numbers and open a second location in Silver Rose, which is on the western side of Avyngäard and further north than Arabelle. The placement will make enforcing anti-magic laws simplier and quicker.

Before joining, we recommend taking a look at what the Church stands for, and what their mission is. Most law abiding and logical people, who understand how dangerous magic is, will fully agree with the Church. Read more about the Church of the Herald on our Government page.

Sonridge Tremors

Severe earthquakes have been radiating from Sonridge. Officials report that they have ceased mining and the Tremors are still occuring.

We are unaware of what is causing these tremors, but it is cause for alarm. There have been multiple reports across the continent that suggest an unknown Elemental Force is causing havoc. Witnesses in Arynsport have reported that tidal waves have been plaguing the city, and our friends in Frostfire have said that an unusual amount of strong blizzards have been occuring.

Cult of the Herald Recruiting Now

The Cult of the Herald is planning on opening another branch in Silver Rose, although we are told that Silver Rose is as against this plan as we are. The cult is dedicated to wiping out magic users and will stop at nothing to complete their mission.

More alarming, there have been reports a secret society within the cult itself, only available to certain members. We have been unable to infiltrate this society as of now. The society calls itself The Exterminators, and boast a rats head as their sigil. They are the most brutal of the Cult's members and have been known to mutilate their victims without question or inquery. Several of their victims have been non-magic users, but simple magicians and entertainers. It is wise to avoid using any form of magic outside the barrier.

Arynsport Heroes

Recently, a group of heroes were able to stop the tidal waves plaguing Arynsport. As Sonridge is also experiencing unsual elemental behavior, we believe it is prudent that we discuss with these heroes what they saw and how they stopped it.

It is reported the heroes are, as far as we known, the following: Savven, a Tiefling; Bindar, a Red Dragonborn; Calypso, a water genasi elf; and Dax, a Satyr. It is unknown which possess magical talents and which do not.

If anyone is able to find these adventurers, last seen in Swallows Song, please bring them to us right away. A reward will be given to anyone who brings them into the Arcane Academy, as well as for the adventures for complying.

Carnaval Du Mystique

The Carnaval Du Mystique is coming to Caster Hills! This circus, who only makes an appearance once a decade, is filled with magic from Feywild. Come see magic like you've never seen it before, performed by exotic magic wielders in an fun and safe environment.

Tickets will be on sale at the ticket booth for 5cp a ticket.

You can expect the Carnaval in about a week. Please meet at the Arcane Academy on opening day if you'd like to travel there as part of a group. It should also be noted that those who do not know where the circus is, will not be able to find it, so it will be safe from the Cult of the Herald.
