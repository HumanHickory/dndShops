Weekly News

Destruction of City Property

Qutoa, Swamp Digest

The Hyenas have struck again destroying several homes and shops within the city. No one knows where these Hyenas have come from, but they seem more intelligent and purposeful than standard beasts. The attacks started 3 moons ago and seem to occur once a week. The shop owner at Epic Flail noted during this attack that he saw a strange shadowy figure accompanying the Hyenas.

All Hail the King!

Bhaokzuju, Swamp Digest

King Thrixl has decided to raise tax by 3% to help fund the creation of a Monument in his own honor. The merciful Lizard King plans to make the statue entirely out of gold. Some suggest this is an extremely bad idea as a giant golden statue may bring the unwanted attention of the Dragon Vydryvion. Since no one has seen the Shadow in 100 years, the handsome king has decided to ignore this advice. Additionally, the King of Kings has beheaded those suggesting he not build this statue to ensure there is not mass hysteria amongst his people.
