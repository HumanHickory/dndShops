History

The city of Oclul was originally the home to a dry grassland people, but the heat of the sun over many centuries converted the grassland into a wasteland, and then into a desert. However, the soft, loose sand is only a few inches thick before reaching a hard, moldable earth.

Mahvo Grassfire was a brilliant traveling merchant whose frequently travelled from Sonridge, the Mining City, to Abydos, the Tiefling Village. One day, on his journey, his foot was swallowed by the sand. When he managed to pull his foot out, it was covered in egg shells and green goo. After some exhausting digging, Mahvo came across an abanoned clutch of thri-kreen eggs. Knowing the abandoned village of Oclul was close by, he transported all the eggs to the village and reburied them in the cracking domes.

The first clutch of eggs only produced about 6 thri-kreen. Mahvo immediately began providing the tiny insects with meat and well water, encouraging them to accept this as their home. Not knowing any differently, the baby insects accepted this as their life. Over time, their numbers began to multiple.

Although Mahvo will occasionally still trade with other civilizations himself, he much prefers to have others come to him, and trade within the city of Oclul. Many traders and merchants are excited to see the city, since the Thri-kreen are rarely seen elsewhere.
