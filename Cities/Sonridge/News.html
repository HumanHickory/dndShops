Tremors!

Annabelle Merryberry, Sonridge Weekly

It's no surprise that Sonridge has been experiencing some nasty tremors as of late. But what could be causing it. Tremor expert Filius Fry suggests purple worms are behind it, an ancient species of giant worm thought to have carved out the entire underdark. Other self-proclaimed experts like Marilyn Marque have even crazier ideas of "tectonic plates", which are incredibly huge pieces of rock that 'float' on underground lava pools and move. She hilariously believes that these occasionally bump into each other, creating seismic activity. What a kook!

What's even more interesting is they're experiencing the same earthquakes in Undrek'thoz, the Drow settlement beneath Sonridge. No one has been able to communicate with them on what they believe is happening.
