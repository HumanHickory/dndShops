Pig

Description: Each round, players will compete to get as close to 21 points as possible. They do this by rolling a d6 as many times as they like, and adding each new roll to their total score. However, if a player rolls a 1, their points for the round reset to 0. Players who get exactly 21 points will win triple the pot (or split evenly if there is a tie). If no one gets 21 points, the player who is the closest (either over or under) will win triple their bet.

Minimum Buy In: 2gp

Gyp

Description: A player rolls 2d6, and if they hit 7 or 12, they win. Players can double their bet and add another 1d6 on a fail. 1.5 payout.
