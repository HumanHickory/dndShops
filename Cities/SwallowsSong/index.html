Swallow's Song

Swallow's Song is a unique town on the western side of the Silent Plains. It is so far west that it is bordered by the Storm's Rage mountains. For thousands of years, Swallow's Song was the last city adventurers would visit before attempting to cross the mountains, as it is is at the bottom of the lowest ridge of the mountain range.

The climate of the village is perfect for apples to grow. The villagers have been proud of their orchards for a thousand years. That is, until the undead came. After destroying major cities such as Silver Rose, the necromancer and her undead army came to Swallow's Song, burning every house and every apple tree, simply to climb the mountain to the Talos Temple.

It is also said that the warriors who defeated the Necromancer first met in Swallow's Song. Although Nosaj, Ellena, and Velhalla all parished, they were able to bring together other adventures, such as the late Vincent and Balsimar, and the heros who were finally able to stop the evil changeling, Willum, Zen, Nym and Faul. It is said that Nym, the most powerful druid who ever lived, even visited Swallow's Song to try our famous apple pie and said it was the best she had ever tasted in her life.

If we can grow apples this good without magic, you don't need magic for anything.

The Orchards

Here is Swallow's Song, we have just about every type of apple you can think of. We have the more common Golden Delicious, Fuji, Gala, Lady, McIntosh, Granny Smith, Honey Crisps and much more. We also have more exotic apples, such as the Black Diamond, the Pink Pearl, and the Java Apple.

We spend 365 days a year tending to our apples and just about every villager has at least one apple tree, even if they're not a farmer. We turn these delicious fruit into even better baked goods and sell them to adventurers attempting to climb the Storm's Rage mountains. We like to give them a little goodness in their life before they die.

Now, with the new lift system designed by gnome tinkerer Zimzamkatan, everyone can travel to Arynsport across the mountains. This has only helped grow our business, and we are now able to trade with Arysnport with ease.

Pumpkins?!

The Whiteapple family is selling their orchard to buy new farmline to grow pumpkins. Yes, you read that right. Pumpkins. What orange abomination is this?

There is still time. If you see a member of the Whiteapple family, please remind them how amazing their apples are and how it just won't be the same eating Whiteapple's Apples without them being grown by the Whiteapple family.

Please also keep in mind how this will impact the economy. If we begin selling Pumpkin Pies, tourists will be confused as to what we are known for. Even worse, some may support the pumpkin fields moreso than the orchards! This would be out of pity of course, not because pumpkin products could ever be better than apple products.

Strange Tremors

The strange vibrations you have been feeling are real. We have word from the east that the city of Sonridge is experiencing unexplainable earthquakes. We believe that we can feel them due to the Crinamorte Mining Co.'s underground tunnel that connects Arynsport to Sonridge.
