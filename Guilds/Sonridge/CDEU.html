Coalition Dedicated to the Eradication of the Undead (CDEU)

Guild Amenities

You have a better chance of not dying against the undead. We may give you other things if you're not useless.

Membership

Anyone can apply to be a member if they have a passion for destroying the undead. Despite the sudden decrease in undead activity, we are still actively recruiting members in case the undead army returns unexpectedly.

Clerici Vitae

A human druid from Willow's Watch, Sandu came to us when his village was destroyed by the undead.

As a dwarf barbarian, Ciprian simply enjoys killing the undead. His blood lust is his only motivation.

Dorian, a rare glimmerfold bard, has been sent from Star Dust in Feywild to deal with the looming evil over the land. He is adamant that the undead problem is just the beginning of our problems.

Once a beautiful Aasmiar, Katarina has denounced her angelic ways and become a warlock. After her angelic guide pushed her to murder her father, a warlock for the Raven Queen, Katarina turned on her guide and fell from grace. She is here because the Raven Queen, her new patron, suggested this was the optimal place to be.

A lightning genasi, Demetra is able to conduct electricity to fuel her arcane magic. Her body is rippled with branching scars, as the electrical current never stops running through her veins.
