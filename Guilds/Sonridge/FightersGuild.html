Guild Amenities

Knights of the Lost Age provides continuous amenities to our members. Although not all amenitites are provided with basic memberships, warriors can move up in ranks and be granted access to benefits that others can only dream of.

  1. Access to local quests with pay
  2. Access to Guild Inn
  3. Access to training facilities (increase strength or gain proficiency in athletics)
  4. Access to pool (increase constitution or double current swim speed)
  5. Access to acrobatics (increase dexterity or gain proficiency in acrobatics)
  6. Discounts on weapons at Epic Flail
  7. Discounts on armor at Ricochet Armaments
  8. Access to be mentored by Higher Ups (potentially learn a new feat)

Membership

Members may join the guild by paying the necessary costs and engaging in combat with a guild-chosen opponent. Member may choose to engage in magical combat or non-magical combat prior to test. Additional rewards may be provided, as available, upon promotion but are not guaranteed. New members may come in at any rank up to Captain but will still be required to complete at least 1 orientation class. You need only pay the entrance fee for the rank you are applying for. Failure to pass any test, including the entrance test, at any level will prevent re-trial for at least 1 month.

Leadership

General Zauviir, Half Drow Half Elf, is said to have studied martial arts since before the first dragons hatched from their eggs. As a disciplined monk, it was his ki that calmed the raging turmoil of the oceans, the relentless rage of the volcanos, and the endless howling of the wind. Now, a man older than time himself, has settled down in Sonridge to teach others the way of the fist. He has been General of The Knights of the Lost Age for over 300 years, and is still going strong.

Aaron Windsbane

Aaron Windsbane, a human ranger from the town of Swallows Song, has spent his life searching the world for rare bows, crossbows, arrows, and bolts. It is said that, even as a boy, he never missed his target with a ranged weapon.

Julius

Julius, a half elf paladin, has studied swordmanship since before most of us were born. He excels at all bladed weapondry, but prefers swords.

Berhsyn

A goliath barbarian, Berhsyn began fighting with his brother, Thyrson, as children. His love for breaking bones with his bare fists quickly advanced him up the ranks at our guild.

Armond Shepard

A Human Fighter, Armond grew up on a farm fighting with wooden swords with his brothers. As the eldest, it was Armond's duty to protect the flock from wolves. Very quickly, he realized he would be much more effective if riding his childhood horse, SugarSnout. From there, it was nearly impossible for Armond's parents to get him off horseback.

Z

A blind human monk, nothing is known of Z's past. He enjoys long hours of silence before battle, and only takes on monk trainees.

Celes

As the only female Master At Arms, Celes is the most commonly challenged Master. However, she has never lost a battle as long as she's been with the guild. As an Elven Eldritch Knight, she is deadly with both weapondry and magic.
