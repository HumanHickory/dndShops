Academy of Mystic Arts

Knowledge and truth can only be truly achieved through magic.

-Ahmera

Guild Amenities

Amenitites are academy exclusive and can only be accessed by members. Not all amenities are available to general members.

  1. Access to local quests with pay
  2. Access to Guild Inn
  3. Access to General Library (learn new languages, look up monsters, etc) and librarians
  4. Access to Arcane Library (increase intelligence or learn 1 spell level 2 or lower)
  5. Access to Divination Lounge (increase wisdom or learn 1 level 4 or lower divination spell)
  6. Access to Auditorium (increase charisma or learn 1 level 4 or lower bard spell)
  7. Discounts on product at Eye of the Beholder
  8. Access to be mentored by Higher Ups (potentially learn a new feat)

Membership

Members may join the guild by paying the necessary costs. All members must have at least minor magical ability but may not be able to ascend to higher ranks without being able to cast higher level spells.

Leadership

An elven sorceress, Ahmera is said to be thousands of years old, but looks no older than a 200 year old elf. Her magic is drawn from every realm and she has been undefeated for many centuries.

Rhyben is the newest member of the counsel and is an air genasi bard. His carefree nature is welcomed in the counsel.

Marina, a Triton Druid from the sea. Although she can attune with all natural power, no one in the world can rival her connection with the natural power of water.

Known for her unfailing ability to heal, this Aasimar Cleric is one with all life forms.

Where there is light, there must be darkness. Cerberin, the Drow Warlock, has bonded with a demon lords in his lifetime, bringing darkness and destruction to the world. Now, he uses that energy to keep his home of Sonridge safe.

An elven wizard, Valera prefers spending her time in the library studying arcane magic and history.

The Academy's museum curator, Aldrin is a human wizard who is not shy to admit that he believes magic users to be better than non-magic users.

Another warlock, Drake has dedicated his magic to serving the arch fey. Although he resides in Sonridge, the Supreme has granted him permission to spend the vast majority of his time in Feywild.

A most unusual counsel member, Scyth is a dragonborn sorcerer from the Desert of Dragons, in the sands of Demon's hearth Desert. It is said that Scyth has worked with ancient dragons to perfect his magic and gain powers that other sorcerers could never dream of.
