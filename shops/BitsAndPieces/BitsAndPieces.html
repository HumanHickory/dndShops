Our Story

Our history begins about 800 years ago. Our goblin ancestors were known for stealing obtaining high quality animals and turning them into meat fit for a king. As years went by, we noticed that keeping that high quality meat fresh was a hassle and began selling the animals alive. This proved more profitable and customers were able to choose when they wanted to eat our products.

We still keep traditions alive. All animals who are unruly, unpurchased for an extended amount of time, or returned will promptly be converted to food.
