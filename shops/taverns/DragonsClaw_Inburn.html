Beverages

Beer 1 gp / pint

Bitter Black A heavy stout. Served at cellar temperature, it is jet black with a dark brown head of foam.
Dragon's Breath A brew of harsh temperament. It is often served with a platter of dark rye bread and strong cheese.
Elminster's Choice A dark, cloudy beer with a heavy head and a smoky aftertaste.
Luiren's Best Brewed by the halflings, this hefty stout is as black as ink and thick. It has a sweet flavor and frothy head.

Wine 2 gp / pint

Arabellan Dry A dry red wine with woodsy undertones and a berrylike taste.
Blood Wine Blood wine has a heavy body and deep-red tone. Its taste is lush and full with a slight after bite, made from shriveled grapes said to be possessed by the dead.
Fire Wine A thick, almost black wine made in Casterhills. It is very strong and spicy, and is said to have medicinal qualities.
Bog Wine Free with purchase of food. Illness or death following consumption is in no way related to the wine.

Mixed Drinks 3 gp / pint

Lizard's Bourbon Bourbon made here in Inburn. Drink it straight or on the rocks.
Visitors Bourbon Lizard's Bourbon watered down, for those who can't handle it straight.
Venom Bourbon made with a small amount of toxin from toads. Causes an enjoyable tingling sensation and sometimes death.
Dragonfly A sweet bourbon with a hint of mint and ground dragonfly.
